Why teams standardize on GtmDB

  • Relationships, not lonely tables

    Accounts, deals, campaigns, and leads stay connected—so agents can explore context, not chase joins across tools.

  • Policies that match reality

    Tenant isolation, read/write rules, field masking, and redaction—different keys for humans, internal bots, and partners.

  • Audit you can use

    Request-level activity logging (including reasoning when callers send it) plus self-serve log access scoped to each actor.

  • Search & traverse

    Full-text search and neighborhood exploration so automations can navigate the funnel without one-off SQL.
